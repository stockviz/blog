# Food: A problem of plenty?

**Blog post:** [Food: A problem of plenty?](https://stockviz.biz/index.php/?p=2103033)

> Charted global food commodity prices (cereals, rice, wheat) against fertilizer prices. Observed that while food prices have barely moved since the 1990s, fertilizer prices remain elevated even when food prices decline, contextualizing persistent farm distress.
