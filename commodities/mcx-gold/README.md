# Understanding Futures Rollover Cost

**Blog post:** [Understanding Futures Rollover Cost](https://stockviz.biz/index.php/?p=40890484)

> Analyzed the rollover cost of holding gold futures (GOLDM) versus gold ETFs (GOLDBEES), finding that contango-induced rollover costs at expiry can erode returns by ~2.5% before fees. Gold ETFs are the clearly superior choice for long-term gold exposure.
