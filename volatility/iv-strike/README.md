# The Smirk

**Blog post:** [The Smirk](https://stockviz.biz/index.php/?p=40890387)

> Examined the volatility smirk (skew) in Indian options markets, where OTM puts trade at higher implied volatility than OTM calls following the 1987 crash pattern. Found the smirk flattens farther from spot, and the real IV surface differs markedly from idealized textbook smiles; also noted rare instances where calls traded at higher IV than puts.
