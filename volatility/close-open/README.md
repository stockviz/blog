# Day vs. Overnight Volatility

**Blog post:** [Day vs. Overnight Volatility](https://stockviz.biz/index.php/?p=40890421)

> Extended overnight volatility analysis across different indices (commodity vs. FMCG stocks, QQQ vs. XLE). Found commodity stocks have higher close-close volatility than FMCG, and QQQ had higher relative overnight volatility than XLE, possibly due to earnings releases occurring outside market hours; weekend risk was surprisingly low relative to daily and overnight risk.
