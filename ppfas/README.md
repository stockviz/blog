# What is the right benchmark for funds owning US equities?

**Blog post:** [What is the right benchmark for funds owning US equities?](https://stockviz.biz/index.php/?p=2101493)

> Argued that mutual funds with US equity carve-outs (like Parag Parikh LTE) should benchmark against a 65/35 Indian Midcap/US Large Cap index rather than broad Indian indices. Found the LTE Fund delivered ~1.8% annualized excess returns over this more appropriate benchmark, in a tax-efficient wrapper.
