# Budget-day Options

**Blog post:** [Budget-day Options](https://stockviz.biz/index.php/?p=40890482)

> Analyzed trading strategies around Indian budget days, finding that 16 of the last 26 budgets ended in the red. Delta-hedged short strangles held overnight and closed on budget day showed decent profit potential, though with significant intraday execution risk.
