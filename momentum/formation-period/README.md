# Momentum Formation Period

**Blog post:** [Momentum Formation Period](https://stockviz.biz/index.php/?p=40890243)

> Tested different formation periods (beyond the standard 12 months) for momentum portfolios. Found no single ideal formation period across all metrics, but the average of top-performing portfolios worked out to 12 months, and equity curves across formation periods were highly correlated.
